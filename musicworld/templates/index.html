Music

LC

2014-01-04

For years I’ve been raving about my great fiery love for this band to pretty much anyone who would listen, and so to see them enjoy such success this year has been a thing of enormous joy for me. I’m not sure they sit so neatly in the Americana bracket, but they’re from Baltimore and I adore them, so I’m claiming them as my own. This of course is the single, and the Letterman performance, that first turned heads back in March. Much has been made of Samuel T Herring as frontman – the bullish frame, the dancing, the ferocity, but behind the impeccable showmanship there lies something incredibly special – music that is heartfelt and guttural and true.
